Gender Bias in the Workforce

In the rapidly evolving landscape of the tech industry, gender bias remains a palpable challenge, casting a shadow over its workforce and organizational structures. Despite the strides made towards workplace equality, gender disparities persist, particularly in technology, science, and engineering. This narrative delves into the nuanced dimensions of gender bias in the tech sector, exploring the historical context, workforce statistics, and the persistent challenges faced by women.
